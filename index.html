About Me
I am an aspiring Solutions Architect looking to break through into the world of AWS. After nearly 10 years in the accountancy industry I found and grew a passion in technology and seeing how automation can transform lives.

I have a lot of experience getting stuck in with clients and thrive when able to talk strategy and finding ways to make their requirements a reality. I am always keen to experiment and find out how new technologies work, and then apply that to make the solutions I implement even better. I have a proven track record of excellent customer service and want to be able to continue to make a positive impact to my clients.

I am currently CCP and SAA Certified and currently studying towards the Terraform Associate certificate, with aims to pursue further and achieve the Security Specialty and Architect Professional certifications.

In my spare time you can find me either watching various motorsport such as Formula 1, Formula E and Indycar amongst others, or working on my homelab and learning more about Linux and the FOSS ecosystem, of which I use Manjaro Linux as my primary OS.

I am confident that I would become a valued member of your team and hope that you will consider me for your next Solutions Architect or DevOps role.
